M.E.D.I.S.
Modular Emergency Drug Intelligent Synthesizer
Autonomous Portable Medicine Formulation Platform

Inventor & Lead Systems Architect: Vishal Nelaturi

---

## Executive Summary

M.E.D.I.S. is a portable chemical synthesis system capable of producing essential medicines in the field.

The platform integrates:

Dosage Engine
Quality Validation Engine
Process Control Engine

These engines coordinate synthesis, verification, and safety management.

---

## Problem Context

Modern healthcare systems depend on fragile global supply chains.

Common failures include:

- supply disruption
- cold chain breakdown
- delayed logistics
- counterfeit drugs

M.E.D.I.S. addresses these limitations by synthesizing drugs locally.

---

## Core System Concept

The system converts raw chemical cartridges into finished liquid medications through controlled synthesis pipelines.

Key capabilities:

- cartridge-based chemical storage
- programmable synthesis
- automated quality validation
- sterile dispensing

---

## System Architecture Overview

Module 1 — Gravity-Fed Cartridge Bank

Cartridge categories include:

API cartridges
Buffer cartridges
Catalyst cartridges
Solvent cartridges

---

Module 2 — API Hydration & Pre-Dissolution

External reservoirs provide:

Sterile water
Ethanol
Saline

Internal reservoirs manage:

Temperature
Turbidity
Fluid levels

---

Module 3 — Precision Syringe Dispensing Core

The dispensing subsystem uses:

Micro-valves
Linear actuators
Pressure sensors
Optical flow sensors

---

Module 4 — Microfluidic Mixing Network

The microfluidic network includes:

Serpentine channels
Passive mixers
Reaction chambers
Filtration membranes

---

Module 5 — Quality Control & Spectroscopy

Sensors monitor:

Temperature
Turbidity
pH level
Optical density

---

Module 6 — Control, Power & Sterilization

Subsystem components:

Embedded compute board
Sterilization UV module
Battery pack
Power regulators
